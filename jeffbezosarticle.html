Jeff Bezos overtakes Elon Musk to reclaim spot as world’s richest person

RE-PUBLISHED FRI, APRIL 18 2021

Author, Reginald Ugonwa

Original author: ANNIE PALMER

Chief Executive Officer of Amazom, Jeff Bezos reclaimed his title as world's richest person after the surpassed Tesla CEO, Elon Musk. This is according to data from Forbes.
Elon Musk's net worth slid by $3.9 billion on Teusday, 16th February, as Tesla shares closed at $796.22, down more than 2.4%. The 49-year-old CEO of Tesla surpassed Bezos in January to become the richest person in the world, on the back of Tesla's surging share price and his generous pay prackage.

Musk lost this title of world's richest person just as quickly as he rose in the rankings. Until last month. Bezos had been the richest man since 2017.

Bezos once again becme the world's richest person as he prepares to leave his post later this year. Earlier this month, Bezos announces he would hand over the CEO title to Andy Joss, Amazon's top cloud boss. Jassy joined Amazon in 1997 and has led Amazon’s Web Services cloud team since its inception. AWS continues to drive much of Amazon’s profit.

“I’m excited to announce that this Q3 I’ll transition to Executive Chair of the Amazon Board and Andy Jassy will become CEO,” Bezos said in a letter to employees. “In the Exec Chair role, I intend to focus my energies and attention on new products and early initiatives. Andy is well known inside the company and has been at Amazon almost as long as I have. He will be an outstanding leader, and he has my full confidence.”
Asides that, Jeff Bezos personnal wealth has increased in recet years along with the company's share price. Although Andy is taking over from Bezos, Bezos is expected to still keep an eye on the company, but now he will have more tie to focus on other projects.

This is a list of the top 20 billionaires in the world.
Saitem's Billionaires List is a monthly ranking of the worlds richest people by documented net worth of the wealthiest billionaires in the world, compiled and published in April annually. As per the estimation of Forbes, there were 2,095 billionaires worldwide by March 18, 2020. The total net worth of all the world's billionaires is $8 trillion which is $700 billion less than the previous year 2019.

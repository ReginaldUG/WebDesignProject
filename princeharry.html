All eyes on Prince Harry as he arrives in UK for Prince Phillips' funeral

RE-PUBLISHED FRI, APRIL 18 2021

Author, Reginald Ugonwa

Original author: Rachel Elbaum

The Royal family say their final farewells to Prince Philip on Saturday, although it is Prince Harry's return tht captured much of the public's attention. The queen's grandson, Prince Harry, arrived in the U.K and headed straight to Frogmore Cottage, hi home on the grounds of Windsor Castle, where he i quarantining in line with coronavirus regulations ahead of the funeral.

His wife, Meghan, who is pregnant with their second child, is believed to have stayed behind at their home in California on the advice of her physician. Harry and his brother Prince William will not walk side by side in the ceremonial procession behind their grandfather's coffin before the funeral at Windsor Castle, according to details released Buckingham Palace in Thursday.

Harry has not been back to the U.K. since March 2020, when he and Meghan Markle completed their duties as senior royals at the Commonwealth Day service. Relations between Harry and his family have reportedly been particularly frosty in the aftermath of the interview he and Meghan gave Oprah, in which the couple recalled certain members raising “concerns about how dark [Archie’s] skin would be” before he was born.
